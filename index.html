The person behind your numbers.

My name is Hunting. I'm a husband, a dad, and a bit of a numbers nerd. I studied Finance at BYU and spent the last 5+ years as a financial analyst working with companies doing over $10M a year in revenue.

I started LedgerHunt because I kept meeting small business owners who were great at what they do but hated dealing with their books. Receipts in a drawer, dreading tax season, no real picture of where the money's going.

I figured if I could help companies manage millions, I could definitely help small businesses get a handle on theirs.

I'm not just someone who categorizes your transactions and calls it a day. I actually dig into your numbers, flag things that don't look right, and help you understand what's going on financially so you can make smarter calls.

Think of me as the finance guy in your corner.
